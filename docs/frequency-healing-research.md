## 1. Binaural Beats & Brainwave Entrainment

### Evidence Level: **STRONG**

#### Key Research

**Systematic Review (PMC10198548, 2023)**
- 14 studies reviewed on brainwave entrainment
- 5 studies confirmed entrainment hypothesis
- Brain does synchronize to beat frequencies
- Source: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10198548/

**Parametric Investigation (Scientific Reports, Feb 2025)**
- 80 participants, factorial design with EEG
- Gamma (40 Hz) with low carrier + white noise improved attention
- EEG confirmed brain entrainment varies with parameters
- Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-88517-z

**Integrative Review (Dec 2024)**
- 84 studies analyzed
- Improvements found in: pain, sleep, mood, cognition
- Need larger sample sizes for definitive conclusions
- Source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39699823/

## 2. 432 Hz Tuning

### Evidence Level: **MODERATE-STRONG**

#### Key Studies

**Health Effects Study (PubMed 31031095, 2019)**
- 33 participants, double-blind crossover
- Results for 432 Hz vs 440 Hz:
  - Heart rate: **-4.79 bpm** (p = 0.05)
  - Blood pressure: slight decrease (not significant)
  - Respiratory rate: -1 breath/min (p = 0.06)
  - Subjective: More focused, more satisfied
- Source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31031095/

**COVID-19 Nurse Study (PubMed 35545982, 2022)**
- Emergency nurses during pandemic
- 432 Hz showed significant reduction in:
  - Heart rate
  - Respiratory rate (p < 0.001)
  - Blood pressure (p = 0.032)
- No effect seen with 440 Hz
- Source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35545982/

**Sleep Study - Spinal Cord Injuries (2020)**
- 12 patients, double-blind crossover
- 432 Hz: Sleep score improved +3.6 (p = 0.02)
- 440 Hz: No improvement (-1.50, p = 0.34)
- Source: https://mattioli1885journals.com/index.php/actabiomedica/article/view/10755

#### Recommendation
**Use 432 Hz as default carrier frequency** - has the strongest evidence for calming effects.

## 3. 528 Hz ("Love Frequency")

### Evidence Level: **MODERATE**

#### Key Studies

**Endocrine/Autonomic Study (SCIRP 87146, 2018)**
- Japanese study, 5 minutes of exposure
- Results:
  - Cortisol: Decreased
  - Oxytocin: Increased
- Source: https://www.scirp.org/journal/paperinformation?paperid=87146

**Anxiety Study (2023)**
- Experimental group showed decreased state anxiety
- Mean reduction: 4.625 points (p = 0.006)
- Suggests activation of relaxation pathways

**Ethanol Toxicity Study (Journal of Addiction Research & Therapy)**
- In vitro study on cells
- 528 Hz reduced toxic effects of ethanol
- Cell life increased by ~20%

#### Caveats
- Most studies are preliminary/pilot
- Mechanisms not fully understood
- "DNA repair" claims lack rigorous evidence

## 6. Audio Quality Best Practices

### Professional Standards

| Parameter | Standard | High | Pro/Audiophile |
|-----------|----------|------|----------------|
| Sample Rate | 44,100 Hz | 48,000 Hz | 96,000 Hz |
| Bit Depth | 16-bit | 24-bit | 32-bit float |
| Peak Level | -3 dBFS | -1 dBFS | -0.1 dBFS |

### Generation Principles

1. **Pure sine waves** - Clinical, precise, good for research replication
2. **Harmonic enrichment** - Fuller sound, more pleasant for extended listening
   - Even harmonics (2nd, 4th, 6th): Warm, musical
   - Odd harmonics (3rd, 5th, 7th): Bright, present
3. **Noise layering** - Pink noise for fuller soundscapes
4. **Proper normalization** - Maximize loudness without clipping
